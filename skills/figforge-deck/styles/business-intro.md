# Style: business-intro (商业介绍 / 公司或产品 pitch)

Target: a customer-facing or investor-facing pitch slide. Bolder, more visual, more
confident than a work report; still tasteful.

---

## Anchors (constant across the deck)

1. **Color palette**
   - base: midnight `#0E1730`
   - secondary: bright cyan `#21C7E8`
   - accent: warm cream `#F5EFE3`

2. **Typography**
   - display: a bold geometric sans (think Inter Tight / Söhne / GT America)
   - body: same family at medium weight
   - never serif

3. **Layout grid**
   - cover / section / closing → full-bleed midnight hero with overlay
   - content → cream background with side decoration

4. **Recurring motif**
   - a thick cyan "corner bracket" ⌐ in the bottom-right of every slide,
     same size and offset throughout

5. **Background type**
   - midnight solid for cover / section / closing
   - cream solid for content

---

## Per-role layout

### cover

- Background: midnight solid
- Title: very large bold geometric sans in cream, left-aligned, with breathing room
- Subtitle (≤1 caption): smaller sans in cyan, beneath the title
- Big cyan corner bracket bottom-right (slightly larger than on other slides)

### section

- Background: midnight with a soft abstract gradient blob in cyan at very low opacity
  (the only place gradients are permitted in the deck)
- Numeral: very large sans "01" / "02" in cream, top-left
- Section title: medium sans, cream, beneath the numeral
- Cyan corner bracket bottom-right

### content

- Background: cream solid
- Title: bold geometric sans in midnight, left-aligned at the top
- Captions (≤2): stacked beneath the title in midnight, smaller sans, each preceded by
  a tiny cyan square or dash
- Right side hosts ONE bold abstract product / concept visual (geometric, slightly
  playful, in midnight + cyan ink). Examples: an isometric block stack, an abstract
  product silhouette, a concept-arrow composition
- Cyan corner bracket bottom-right (in midnight color this time, since background is cream)

### closing

- Background: midnight
- Centered or left-aligned cream phrase: "Let's talk" / "Get in touch" / "Thank you"
- A placeholder mark for contact (e.g., cyan dash + cyan dash) — never bake a real email,
  phone number, or social-media handle into the image
- Cyan corner bracket bottom-right

---

## Visual element voice (the right side of content)

- Prefer bold abstract product silhouettes, mockup shapes, isometric geometric forms,
  concept-arrow compositions
- Prefer confident, high-contrast, simplified
- Avoid: stock-photo people, cliché handshake imagery, generic "Earth with network",
  generic light-bulb-equals-idea, suit-and-tie figures

---

## What NOT to do

- No three-icon row across the slide (corporate cliche)
- No literal "growth chart up and to the right"
- No glossy / chrome / fake 3D
- No multi-stop rainbow gradients (the cyan-on-midnight blob in section is the only
  permitted gradient)
- No drop shadows on text
- No accent line directly under the title
