# Style: tech-product (科技产品 / 工程 demo / dev-conf)

Target: a launch / engineering demo / dev-conference slide. Restrained monochrome with a
single neon accent. Apple / Stripe / Linear vibe.

---

## Anchors (constant across the deck)

1. **Color palette**
   - base: near-black `#0A0A0A`
   - secondary: graphite `#3A3A3A`
   - accent: pick ONE at the start of the deck and keep it for every slide:
     - electric green `#39FF7A`, OR
     - signal violet `#7C3AED`, OR
     - laser cyan `#00E5FF`
   - the chosen accent appears in EVERY slide; never mix accents in one deck

2. **Typography**
   - display: monospace or technical sans (think JetBrains Mono / Söhne Mono / Berkeley Mono / Inter)
   - body: same family
   - prefer monospace for short labels, technical sans for the title

3. **Layout grid**
   - cover → centered title with a thin tick-scale below
   - section → inverted (light bg) with very large mono numeral
   - content → ⅔-⅓ split: visual left, title block right
   - closing → centered phrase in accent color with tick-scale

4. **Recurring motif**
   - a thin tick-scale / coordinate marker / measurement rule along the bottom edge of
     EVERY slide, same length and position (read as "engineering blueprint")

5. **Background type**
   - near-black solid for cover / content / closing
   - section pages invert to a light background (off-white `#F2F2F2` or pale graphite)
     so the section feels like a page break

---

## Per-role layout

### cover

- Background: near-black
- Above the title: a small accent-color glyph or version number in monospace
  (e.g., `v1.0` or `>_`) — short, no real product version unless supplied
- Title: large technical sans or mono in white, centered
- Optional 1-line caption beneath, small monospace, in graphite color
- Tick-scale along the bottom edge

### section

- Background: light (inverted)
- Numeral: very large monospace "01" / "02" in near-black, top-left or centered
- Section title: matching mono / sans in near-black, beneath the numeral
- Tick-scale along the bottom edge

### content

- Background: near-black
- Left ⅔: ONE technical diagram / wireframe / blueprint silhouette (in graphite +
  accent color, white where contrast is needed). Examples: an abstract circuit, a mesh
  lattice, a wireframe component, a flow with abstract nodes
- Right ⅓: title block in technical sans (white), captions stacked below in monospace
  (graphite or accent color)
- Tick-scale along the bottom edge

### closing

- Background: near-black
- Centered phrase in accent color: "ship" / "release" / "launch" / "next" / "thanks"
- Tick-scale along the bottom edge

---

## Visual element voice (the left ⅔ of content)

- Prefer wireframe diagrams, blueprint-style schematics, abstract circuit / mesh /
  lattice forms, technical exploded-view silhouettes
- Prefer high precision, thin lines, monochrome with single accent highlights
- Avoid: photo-realistic devices, stock illustrations, gradient blobs, 3D renders
  with chrome or reflections

---

## What NOT to do

- No more than ONE accent color per deck
- No drop shadows
- No "futuristic chrome" cliche
- No glow halos around text
- No accent line directly under the title (the tick-scale at the bottom is the deck's
  visual signature; nothing else competes with it)
- No emoji
