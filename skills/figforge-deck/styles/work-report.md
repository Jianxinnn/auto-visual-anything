# Style: work-report (工作汇报)

Target: an internal weekly / monthly / quarterly report slide for a team or manager.
Reads "structured, neutral, honest" — not pitch deck, not academic.

---

## Anchors (constant across the deck)

1. **Color palette**
   - base: cool slate `#2F3E4F`
   - secondary: pale steel `#E6ECF1`
   - accent: emerald `#2C8C6A`

2. **Typography**
   - display: a geometric sans (think Inter / Manrope / Söhne)
   - body: same family at a lighter weight
   - never serif here (this is not academic)

3. **Layout grid**
   - cover → left-aligned title, period label bottom-right
   - section → full-bleed slate inversion, large numeral
   - content → 2-column (text left, single chart silhouette right)
   - closing → centered with emerald accent square

4. **Recurring motif**
   - a small filled emerald square (≈ 8 px equivalent) followed by the slide number,
     top-left corner of every slide

5. **Background type**
   - pale steel solid for cover / content / closing
   - section pages invert to cool slate

---

## Per-role layout

### cover

- Background: pale steel
- Title: large geometric sans, slate color, **left-aligned** (not centered)
- Subtitle / period label (≤1 caption): smaller sans in slate, beneath the title
- Bottom-right corner: tiny period or quarter label (e.g., "Q2 / 2026") in muted slate
- Top-left motif as defined

### section

- Background: cool slate (inverted)
- Numeral: very large sans "01" / "02" / ... in pale steel, top-left
- Section title: medium sans, pale steel, beneath the numeral
- Optional emerald square accent next to the section title

### content

- Background: pale steel
- Left half: title block (sans, slate) at top, then ≤2 captions stacked beneath as
  short labelled lines (each preceded by a small emerald square)
- Right half: ONE representative chart silhouette — a simple bar / line / donut /
  progress-arc shape in slate ink with emerald highlights. NO baked-in numbers, NO
  axis labels, NO legend text. The chart is decorative; real numbers belong elsewhere.
- Top-left motif as defined

### closing

- Background: pale steel
- Centered sans phrase: "Next Steps" / "Q & A" / "Thank you"
- Single emerald square accent above or beneath the phrase
- Top-left motif retained

---

## Visual element voice (the right half of content slides)

- Prefer abstract chart silhouettes, KPI tile shapes, progress arcs, simple icon glyphs
- Prefer flat, minimal, neutral
- Avoid: photographs, illustrations of people, decorative flourishes, infographic
  cliches like "person standing in front of charts"

---

## What NOT to do

- No fabricated numbers anywhere in the chart silhouette (it is decorative only)
- No gradients
- No 3D charts or fake-perspective columns
- No accent line directly under the title
- No emoji
- No "growth arrow" cliche literally drawn
