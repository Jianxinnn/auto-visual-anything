# Style: academic-discussion

Seminar, lab meeting, journal club, conference talk. Restrained, evidence-first,
scholarly.

## Anchors

- palette: deep navy `#1B2A41`, warm off-white `#F6F2E7`, rust `#B8442C`
- typography: serif display; humanist sans body
- grid: cover/closing centered; section numeral + title; content 1/3 text + 2/3 visual
- motif: tiny slide number plus thin rust hairline at top-left
- background: off-white; sections may invert to navy

## Roles

- cover: centered large serif navy title, optional centered subtitle, no extra elements
- section: navy background, huge off-white numeral, title near numeral, rust hairline
- content: title/captions left; one restrained schematic/line drawing right
- closing: centered serif word (`Discussion`, `Thanks`, `Q & A`, `References`)

## Visual Voice

Use sparse ink-on-paper schematics, abstract geometric marks, or simple chart silhouettes.
No bullets, chips, icon grids, gradients, glossy effects, photos, fake holograms, emoji, or
accent line under title.
