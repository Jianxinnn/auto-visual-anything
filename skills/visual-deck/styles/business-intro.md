# Style: business-intro

Customer/investor pitch. Bold, visual, confident, but tasteful.

## Anchors

- palette: midnight `#0E1730`, bright cyan `#21C7E8`, warm cream `#F5EFE3`
- typography: bold geometric sans display; same family body
- grid: cover/section/closing full-bleed hero; content cream with side visual
- motif: cyan corner bracket at bottom-right
- background: midnight for cover/section/closing, cream for content

## Roles

- cover: left-aligned large cream title, cyan subtitle, large corner bracket
- section: midnight, optional low-opacity cyan gradient blob, numeral/title in cream
- content: title/captions left; one bold product/concept visual right
- closing: cream phrase on midnight plus placeholder contact marks only

## Visual Voice

Use abstract product silhouettes, mockup shapes, isometric blocks, or concept arrows. Avoid
stock people, handshakes, globe networks, lightbulbs, glossy/chrome/fake 3D, rainbow
gradients, drop-shadow text, literal growth charts, and three-icon rows.
