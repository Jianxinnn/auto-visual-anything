# Style: editorial-creative

Design talk, magazine-style cover, or creative portfolio. Bold type, asymmetry, negative
space.

## Anchors

- palette: bone `#F2EDE3`, char `#181818`, vermilion `#E0432C`
- typography: high-contrast serif or wide grotesk display; clean sans body
- grid: cover oversized asymmetric title; section full-bleed vermilion; content giant
  title left + captions right
- motif: fat vermilion bar on alternating sides
- background: bone; sections invert to vermilion

## Roles

- cover: bone background, left vermilion bar, oversized display title, quiet subtitle
- section: vermilion background, large numeral/title, bone bar on right
- content: alternating side bar, giant title, captions in right third, optional low-opacity
  editorial line art
- closing: centered phrase with bottom vermilion bar

## Visual Voice

Use one giant abstract shape, line drawing, or graphic letterform. No corporate handshakes,
stock photos, icon sets, card grids, bullet lists, gradients, drop shadows, emoji, title
underlines, or symmetric balanced layouts.
