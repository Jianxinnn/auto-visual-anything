# Style: literature-review

Survey/review talk. Reads as library, archive, or index-card structure.

## Anchors

- palette: parchment `#EFE8D8`, ink `#1F1B16`, ochre `#B8862C`
- typography: transitional serif display; slab/mono serif body
- grid: cover as journal cover; section as timeline ribbon; content as index-card grid
- motif: small filled circle plus hairline at top-right
- background: subtle parchment texture, flat and low contrast

## Roles

- cover: centered serif title with ochre rule above; optional small subtitle
- section: title above a 3-5 marker timeline ribbon; optional caption below
- content: title across top; 3-column index cards; captions become column subheaders
- closing: centered word with ochre hairlines above/below

## Visual Voice

Use timelines, taxonomic trees, citation-network silhouettes, and flat card grids. No fake
reference text, gradients, 3D, photos, glow, drop shadows, checkmark bullets, emoji, or
corporate icon rows.
