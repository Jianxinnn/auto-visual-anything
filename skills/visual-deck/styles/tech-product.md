# Style: tech-product

Launch, engineering demo, developer conference. Restrained monochrome with one neon
accent.

## Anchors

- palette: near-black `#0A0A0A`, graphite `#3A3A3A`, one accent: `#39FF7A` or `#7C3AED`
  or `#00E5FF`
- typography: monospace or technical sans display/body
- grid: cover centered; section inverted light; content 2/3 visual + 1/3 text
- motif: bottom tick-scale / coordinate ruler
- background: near-black except inverted section pages

## Roles

- cover: small monospace glyph/version if supplied, centered title, tick-scale bottom
- section: light background, huge mono numeral and title
- content: wireframe/blueprint/flow visual left; title/captions right
- closing: centered accent phrase with tick-scale

## Visual Voice

Use wireframes, blueprint schematics, circuit/mesh/lattice forms, thin lines, and one accent
highlight. Avoid multiple accents, photoreal devices, chrome, glow halos around text,
gradient blobs, drop shadows, emoji, and title underlines.
