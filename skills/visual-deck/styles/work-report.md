# Style: work-report

Internal weekly/monthly/quarterly report. Structured, neutral, honest.

## Anchors

- palette: cool slate `#2F3E4F`, pale steel `#E6ECF1`, emerald `#2C8C6A`
- typography: geometric sans for display and body
- grid: cover left-aligned; section full-bleed slate; content 2-column
- motif: emerald square plus slide number at top-left
- background: pale steel; sections invert to slate

## Roles

- cover: left-aligned title and period/subtitle, optional bottom-right period label
- section: huge pale-steel numeral on slate, section title below
- content: title and ≤2 caption lines left; one chart/KPI/progress silhouette right
- closing: centered phrase with emerald square accent

## Visual Voice

Use abstract charts, KPI tiles, progress arcs, and flat icon glyphs. Do not render
fabricated chart numbers, legends, photos, people illustrations, gradients, 3D charts,
growth-arrow cliches, emoji, or title underline.
